Burger

Description

Satisfy your hunger with this juicy, southern-style burger.

This is our classic #1 seller and leaves you craving for more after the first bite.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Season the beef patties
  2. Cook the patties on a hot skillet
  3. Toast the buns in a different skillet
  4. Add mayo to the buns once they are finsihed
  5. Add the cooked burger onto the buns
  6. Add the toppings to the burger
  7. Enjoy!
