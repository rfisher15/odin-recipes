Chicken Tenders

Description

You can't go wrong with our crispy buttermilk chicken tenders

Our classic recipe leave you with a amazing crunch after every bite.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken tenders
  2. Soak the season tenders in a bowl of buttermilk
  3. Create a bowl that has flour
  4. Create another bowl that has egg wash
  5. Take the tenders and coat them in the flour
  6. Remove the tenders from the flour and coat them in the egg wash
  7. Remove the tenders from the egg wash and coat them in the flour once more
  8. Fry the tenders in a pot of hot oil until they are golden brown
  9. Enjoy!
