Lasagna

Description

Try our delicious lasagna and find out what your taste buds have been missing.

This flavorful dish is everything you would want and need in a perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Beef
  • Sausage
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Cottage Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and the sausage in the same pot
  2. Once the meat is fully cooked, add the tomato sauce with the meta
  3. Cook the noodles in boiling water
  4. Mix the cottage cheese and eggs into a bowl
  5. Get an oven safe pan and layer the cooked noodles into the pan
  6. Next, layer the meat sauce mixture on top of the cooked noodles
  7. Then, layer the cottage cheese mixture on top of the meat sauce
  8. Finally, layer the mozerella cheese on top of the cottage cheese mixture
  9. Repeat the layering steps until the pan is full
  10. Cook the dish in the oven at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 45 minutes
  11. Enjoy!
